Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Who we are

This website is operated under the brand A-Brand. For privacy-related requests, please use the contact form available on a-brand.org.

2. What information we collect

  • Name, email address and message content submitted voluntarily through the contact form.
  • Technical and aggregated usage data (when analytics is enabled with consent).

3. Purpose of processing

  • To respond to inquiries and communication requests.
  • To operate and secure the website.
  • To analyze aggregated website usage and improve performance (subject to consent where required).

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

Where applicable under EU law, data processing is based on:

  • Article 6(1)(b) — steps prior to entering into a contract (when discussing services).
  • Article 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest (website operation and security).
  • Article 6(1)(a) — consent (analytics cookies and similar technologies where required).

5. Analytics & cookies

This website may use analytics tools to understand aggregated usage patterns.

Where required by applicable law (including within the European Union), analytics cookies are activated only after user consent via the cookie banner displayed on the website.

You may accept or decline analytics cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser storage or revisiting the site settings.

6. Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond and for reasonable follow-up. Aggregated analytics data is retained according to analytics configuration settings.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. The website may rely on trusted service providers (such as hosting or analytics providers) strictly for operational purposes.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise your rights, please contact us via the website contact form.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect data. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure.

10. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be published on this page.
